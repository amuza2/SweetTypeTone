# Bundled Sound Packs

This directory contains community-made sound packs that are bundled with SweetTypeTone.

## Included Sound Packs (OGG/WAV only)

- **Bruh** - Fun sound pack
- **Cherry MX Black (ABS)** - Cherry MX Black switches with ABS keycaps
- **Cherry MX Black (PBT)** - Cherry MX Black switches with PBT keycaps
- **Cherry MX Blue (ABS)** - Cherry MX Blue switches with ABS keycaps
- **Cherry MX Blue (PBT)** - Cherry MX Blue switches with PBT keycaps
- **Cherry MX Brown (ABS)** - Cherry MX Brown switches with ABS keycaps
- **Cherry MX Brown (PBT)** - Cherry MX Brown switches with PBT keycaps
- **Cherry MX Red (ABS)** - Cherry MX Red switches with ABS keycaps
- **Cherry MX Red (PBT)** - Cherry MX Red switches with PBT keycaps
- **EG Crystal Purple** - Everglide Crystal Purple switches
- **EG Oreo** - Everglide Oreo switches
- **Fallout Terminal** - Fallout-themed terminal sounds
- **NK Cream** - NovelKeys Cream switches
- **Topre Purple Hybrid (PBT)** - Topre Purple Hybrid switches with PBT keycaps

## Source

These sound packs are from the Mechvibes community and are free to use.
